Widget Scopes

SNKit has logged all Service Portal widget AngularJS scope objects to the Service Portal Widget Scopes sidebar of the Elements panel.


1. Open the Elements panel at the top of the devtools window.

2. From the sidebar panels, locate the Service Portal Widget Scopes panel. You may need to show all sidebar panels with the arrow button.

3. Expand each widget to view the scope data.
